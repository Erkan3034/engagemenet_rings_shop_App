About Us

With over 60 years of experience, we are engagement ring specialists who witness love's most special moments and offer quality, unique designs.

Our Story

Our journey began in 1960 in a small workshop and continues today as one of Turkey's most trusted engagement ring brands. With the craftsmanship tradition passed down through three generations, we reflect the purest form of love in every ring.

By combining traditional handcrafting techniques with modern design understanding, we create not just jewelry, but life stories. Each ring is carefully crafted as a tangible symbol of a couple's love story.

Our Philosophy

For us, every ring is not just metal and stone; it's a heritage to be passed to future generations, a carrier of memories, and an eternal symbol of love. With this understanding, we aim for perfection in every piece we create without compromising quality.

With the awareness of responsibility that comes from witnessing our customers' most special moments, we use only the highest quality materials and meticulously process every detail. Because we are creating a symbol of lifelong love.
